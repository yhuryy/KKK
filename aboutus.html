Federation Payatas Youth Leaders

HISTORY

The Federation of Payatas Youth Leaders is a dynamic group of youth parliamentarians who emerged from the 1st Payatas Youth Parliament, a three-day program spearheaded by the award-winning Sangguniang Kabataan of Barangay Payatas that concluded on April 13, 2025.

Throughout the program, these young leaders exhibited a deep commitment to understanding parliamentary procedures by actively attending sessions, applying legislative protocols, and crafting numerous proposed resolutions and ordinances. Their engagement in reflective, respectful debates highlighted their readiness to lead with purpose.

Now, guided by the principles of ethical leadership and a shared dedication to youth empowerment, they are taking their first steps in mobilizing as a force for positive change in their community.

MISSION

The Federation of Payatas Youth Leaders promotes participatory governance, skill-building, and the cultivation of civic pride and responsibility among the youth of Barangay Payatas. The FPYL empowers the voices of every youth in the community, ensuring they are heard, valued, and actively involved in shaping their future. By fostering the development of ethical and visionary leaders, the FPYL aims to inspire a generation that leads with integrity and purpose. The FPYL also strives to unify youth organizations across Barangay Payatas, creating a collaborative network that strengthens solidarity, amplifies impact, and drives meaningful change for and by the youth.

VISION

The organization envisions empowering the youth of Barangay Payatas to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to their community. The FPYL strives to create a space where every youth in Payatas is connected, represented, and supported. Through an active and resilient community, FPYL aims to ensure that all youth have access to opportunities that foster growth, inclusion, and empowerment.
