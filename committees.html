STANDING COMMITTEES

Committee on Active Citizenship, Good Governance, and Sports Development

This committee engages young people in civic activities to promote active citizenship and good governance, while also organizing sports programs to foster teamwork and leadership skills.

Committee on Economic Empowerment, Livelihood, and Global Mobility

This committee champions environmental sustainability, disaster resilience, and sustainable agriculture, supporting local farming and entrepreneurship.

Committee on Education, Arts and Culture, and Social Inclusion and Equity

This committee promotes education, arts, and culture while advocating for inclusion and equity, ensuring all, especially the marginalized, can thrive and contribute.

Committee on Health, and Peace Building and Security

This committee serves as a backbone for public health and wellness initiatives, while also enhancing peace-building and security to ensure community safety and harmony.

Committee on Environment, Disaster Resilience, and Agriculture

This committee is concerned with economic empowerment through the provision of skills training and entrepreneurship development.
